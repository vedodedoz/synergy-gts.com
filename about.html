Who We Are

Canadian Enterprise Consulting Leader

Synergy-GTS is a premier Canadian technology consulting firm specializing in SAP solutions, enterprise architecture, and digital transformation. Founded in 2010, we have grown from a focused SAP practice into a full-service enterprise consulting partner serving organizations across Canada, the United States, and international markets.

Our team of certified SAP consultants, cloud architects, and industry specialists combine deep technical expertise with a genuine understanding of business challenges. We don't just implement technology — we architect solutions that drive measurable outcomes and sustainable competitive advantage.
