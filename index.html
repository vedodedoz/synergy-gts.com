Know About Synergy GTS

Today more than ever, the buzz in the business world is CHANGE, companies struggle to manage that CHANGE affecting their business bottom line. It all started in 2010, a thought drove TEAM Synergy GTS into SAP Consulting world, when client engagement was relatively challenging our team successfully connected great people and organizations. We did something that too few consulting firms are known for today; We started building technology relationships. The thought was that, you could build a better business if you went out of your way to do right for both your clients and your consultants. We develop rich software applications, implement ERP applications, deliver analytics solutions, connect expert human resources with best clients realizing technology goals.

Synergy GTS is a enterprise Consulting Services Provider with expertise in SAP ECC, S/4HANA, SAP Treasury & Risk Management, SAP BTP, SAP Cloud, SAP Basis, Software Services, and Staffing Services. We consistently and efficiently plan, execute, and deliver high-quality services. With 20+ years in SAP strategy, enterprise architecture, and digital transformation leadership across manufacturing, energy, and retail sectors. We enable end-to-end SAP enterprise solutions like SAP S/4HANA, SAP ECC, SAP and deliver end-to-end SAP BTP solutions.

Synergy GTS combines business and technology expertise to bring together the optimum mix of experience, capabilities, consulting, software and utilizing a rapid approach to fulfill business needs. We help customers with strategy definition, value realization, technology implementation, human resource and solution delivery. Backed by proven expertise and experience of our associates and strategic collaborations, who possess vast application consulting experience, we offer technology and business solutions.
